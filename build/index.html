I waste my time creating random computer things.

What I've done so far:

  • TypeWriter.tw

    TypeWriter.tw is a text editor for the web that lets you draft, collect and share your writings.

  • The Road to Battleborn for The Killers

    Take your friends on a roadtrip and listen to the new The Killers album. Also built together with Lee Martin.

  • The Social Connectome for Muse

    A visualization of social connections showing how Muse fans discovered the new Muse album through their friends. Built together with Lee Martin.

  • The Adventures of Stan

    The Adventures of Stan was my first experiment to build a little adventure. The artwork is blatantly stolen and the storyline work in progress.

  • Ency.cl/opedia

    Ency.cl/opedia is an experiment to improve the Wikipedia reading experience. It's focus is on a clutter free, simple design and features that improve exploration and curation. I wrote a bit more on my motivation behind this project on my blog.

  • StoryWheel

    Create old-school slideshows from your instagram pictures and tell the story around them. It was inspired by the carousel pitch from Mad Men. Here are my favorite stories submitted by users: 1 2 and 3.

  • Operation: lolcat.biz

    After a pivot lolcat.biz turned into an incubator to breed out all my new personal projects and tell the world about them. Read and follow the blog.

  • SoundCloud Javascript SDK

    While working at SoundCloud I've created a Javascript SDK, similar to the Facebooks SDK, that provides access to the API but also brings audio recording and streaming capabilities.

  • Waveform.js

    Waveform.js makes drawing SoundCloud waveforms simple and lets you style and color them the way you want it. It comes as a small JavaScript library and is using a lightweight service hosted on waveformjs.org that translates the waveform images provided by SoundCloud into floating points.

  • Recorder.js

    Recorder.js is a lightweight Adobe Flash tool, wrapped with a Javascript API, to record and upload audio in the browser.

  • Concert 2021

    Ever wondered how concerts will look like in 2021? Concert 2021 is the answer. And it even made it to the wired.com frontpage!

  • HTTMultiParty

    HTTMultiParty is a ruby gem that wraps around HTTParty to support multipart uploads.

  • FavoriteColor.co

    Everybody has a favorite color! Pick yours, share it with your friends or install it as an app on your phone.

  • ponyScript

    Write, run & share server-side javascripts on ponyScript.

  • ponyHo.st

    A simple webservice and ruby gem to create and maintain static websites on your Amazon S3 account. It takes 2 simple commands to get a site up and running under a *.ponyho.st domain.

  • The original lolcat.biz

    What lolcat.biz before the pivot was: loltent in its longest and purest form.

  • Mindmap

    One of my early hacks. A realtime collaborative mindmap tool.
